About our coffee

Our coffee bar uses only Ethiopian coffee. All
commercially produced coffee originates from Ethiopia.
We love Ethiopia for providing the world with coffee to
grow, and we continue to love the flavor of that country's
coffee. The fruity acidity of this single origin coffee is
balanced by its sweet honey notes, creating soft, light
notes with a light character.
